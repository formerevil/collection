Our Promise

Our goal when making this website was to highlight creatives and bring their messages to the public. We promise to express the importance of art in the modern world. The messages of each featured creator may not be equal, but we hope the intention of our collection is clear.

FAQ

→ How do you decide what art to feature?

We choose our art based on what art moves us toward our goal of spreading the beauty of art around the world. If your art is beautiful and without hate or harmful messages, we are sure to feature it!
